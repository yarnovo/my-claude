## Slide 8

# Transactions

Purchase – ok

R/T-		One borrower must be on title at time of application
		 List for sales must be withdrawn PRIOR to application
		Use appraised value regardless the purchase date.

Cash Out – 	Current mortgage to be paid off must have 12 months seasoning
		One borrower must be on title for 6 months (prior to closing)
		List for sales must be withdrawn PRIOR to application

Delayed financing -	property purchased within 6 months
			priced as cash out

## Slide 12

# Income

Self Employed –
P&L prepared by tax preparer (CPA, EA, CTEC, PTIN) or by borrower.
Application date before 7/1 – need YTD and last year (12 mos) P&L
Application date after 6/30 – need YTD P&L
P&L must be dated as MM/DD/YYYY – MM/DD/YYYY
Tax preparer letter (on formal letter head) confirming
filed borrower’s last 2 years business tax returns
Confirm borrower has been in business for 2 years or more at the same location(must disclose company name and address)
Confirm borrower’s business ownership %
Tax preparer license may be verified
		AND
2 Years of current business license if using borrower prepared P&L.
Acceptable business license: a: Professional license, e.g. Real Estate License, Cosmetologist license, Insurance license, Massage therapist license, etc; b: City business license.
